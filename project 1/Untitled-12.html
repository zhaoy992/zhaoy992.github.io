Meeting Point

BY LOUIS MACNEICE

WEIBO

I chose Weibo as my first platform because it creates an amazing and public place for everybody to share.
Here is a link to my Weibo homepage.

WECHAT

Wechat is the second platform I posted the poem. Instead of sharing with everybody, it is only sharing with your friends. It gives us a private and close community to chat and post.
Since Wechat creates a private community between friends only, I do not have a link for this page.

TIKTOK

Tiktok is the last platform. It creates public space. The concept of the app is similar to the poem, since people on Tiktok are "meeting" with each of the video for a short moment, just like time meets each of us. And it will eventually go away no matter who we are and what we do.
